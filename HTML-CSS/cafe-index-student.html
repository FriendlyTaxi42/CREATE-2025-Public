Café Central: City of Toronto

In the heart of Toronto, discover curated reviews that highlight the best in coffee, ambiance, and local flavor. From cozy corners to modern brews, we guide you to the city’s top spots for your next study spot or relaxing break - perfect for locals and visitors alike.

  1. Bloom Café

    2. Bloom Café on Yonge offers a minimalist, serene space with Japanese-inspired desserts like earl grey cream puffs and mochi. Enjoy premium teas and lattes in a cozy, light-filled setting perfect for quiet breaks or studying.

  2. Tsujiri

    3. Tsujiri on Dundas Street West offers a serene, minimalist space rooted in Japanese tea tradition. Known for its authentic matcha, it’s a must-visit for those seeking a genuine and refined green tea experience.

  3. MATCHA MATCHA

    4. Matcha Matcha on Church Street is a sleek, modern café known for its creative matcha-based drinks. With a minimalist vibe and self-serve kiosks, it’s a go-to spot for matcha lovers seeking something refreshingly different.

  4. Little Pebbles

    5. Little Pebbles is a cozy café known for its warm atmosphere and exceptional croissants made with rich European butter. It’s the perfect spot to relax, enjoy delicious pastries, and savor specialty coffees in the city.

  5. 18 Feet Espresso Bar & The Cheong

    6. 18 Feet Espresso Bar & The Cheong is a vibrant café blending Korean flavors with modern coffee culture. Known for innovative drinks and a cozy atmosphere, it’s a favorite spot for those seeking a unique and flavorful experience.
